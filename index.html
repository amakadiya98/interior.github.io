    About us

    Schenk Construction Group was established in 2014 by founder Jack Meridjanian. With a collective experience of over 30 years and in-depth knowledge of the latest building technologies, our dedicated team strives to provide the highest quality of workmanship through excellence in communication, integrity, reliability, construction and management from concept to completion

    Our expertise covers residential and commercial projects, including: new builds, refurbishments; alterations; multi-unit developments, dual occupancy and commercial fit outs. Schenk Construction Group is committed to providing impeccable solutions for any sized budget.

    Our team of highly skilled and experienced professionals work closely with our clients to provide a seamless, stress-free building experience. The projects we complete not only surpass our clients' expectations but are also a huge accomplishment for us as a team, in which we take great pride.

    We would love to be a part of your next building project. Contact us to learn more about how we can help bring your vision to life!
